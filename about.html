Hi iam Rahul

I'm a Product Designer passionate about solving problems at scale for millions of people and creating ease to use products.

I am currently a junior pursuing my undergrad as a bachelor in Electronics and Electrical engineering and minoring in Computer science with Machine learning-related concepts.

I started my career as a child observing a small pebble of stone under a waterfall making water dance. My first instinct after observing this minute detail was to find out who else observed it. I liked how different people see the same thing from different perspectives. This led me to explore the field of front-end development without knowing there exists a field called UX. After a few side projects, I fell in love with the why behind problem-solving and the process of finding a solution.

I like to think of myself as a product designer who can lead a project from start to end while enjoying the process behind it.
