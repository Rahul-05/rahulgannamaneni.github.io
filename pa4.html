Google Maps back seat

Overview

PASSION PROJECT • UX DESIGN • New Feature

New back seat feature to existing google maps to allow users to find a better use even when users' are not driving or following directions. And addressing women traveling safety issues.

Roles & Responsibilities

• User Research: Surveys, Competitive Analysis, Interviews, User testing, Affinity Mapping
• Storytelling & Narrative
• UX Design: sketches, Iterative Wireframing, low and high fidelity prototyping, Testing Sessions

Project Context

• Solo Project
• March 2022 -1.5 week

Tools Used

• Figma
• Miro

The Problem

Google maps are the most popularly used maps app with it also being supplied as default maps for android phones. User groups who don't drive but travel frequently using public transport or cab or not been recognized as maps users but users are using maps even in the back seat by using it as default destination router to track their visit from the back seat to not miss their stop just by muting the voice for directions.

However, It's only used by some people who realized it can be used like that but others dint find the purpose to see maps like that so our task is to make this currently used unofficial feature into a fully functional feature with consistent user flow to make new users get better usage of this feature.

Process

User research

1. I conducted interviews with 24 invidual(20 Frequent travelers(Public transport) and 4 Students) and created empathy maps to understand the users.
2. Primary user groups mentioned that they frequently miss there stop when they use public transport.
3. This user group confirmed initial assumptions about the Maps app, but research also revealed people never thought a maps as use when they are not the person driving.
4. Students use public transport frequently to travel to college or any other fests but they mentioned that the problem of missing destination is subtle but some used a means of contacting driver to mention them when they specifically reached their destination but it dint work every time.
5. Other user groups identified are emergency travel like doctors or nurses to reach their destination who mentioned they concentrate on contact with a doctor at the site, so they may miss their stop.
6. Women users across the globe are not allowed to travel at night even it is necessary due to safety issues.

User group-1: Frequent travelers

• For frequent travelers they would like to rest every time they get time to relive the stress of traveling.
• But this frequently leads to missing the destination and picking a cab to reach the destination which in terms takes more money and time.

User group-1: Women travelers

• The problem exists when women travelers travel at night, they are not allowed to travel at night even it is necessary due to incidents like statutory offenses and their won't be any help when they travel at night.
• In some cases, woman travelers are necessary at night who are doctors or have any emergency issues but there is always some terror that makes them feel unsafe.

Research Insights

Nothing to alert the user

Even though most public transports have stop alerts but upon resarch it was noticed that most of them dosent work. Users need a way to make them alert when they are about to reach there destination.

Women users safety

Due to incidents like statutory offenses frequently happening across the place women need better safety precautions to travel tension less.

What means do people use to commence for these problems?

1. Most travelers try to use google maps while silencing the sound and check every time they feel like they need to but this is not effective.
2. Travellers try to be awake most of the time to concentrate on the road but this is odd when they are not driving and cant take a rest.
3. some depend on the neighboring passenger to mention them.

Personas

Feasible solutions

New feature - Back seat mode

• Back seat mode is a feature that can available to choose from when starting their trip after picking a destination.
• This feature doesn't shout out any directions to travel this follows the user with GPS and alerts the user before the user's chosen time from the selected destination.

Auto alert calls on too much deviation from original destination

• Back seat also alerts users on too much deviation from shortest route to destination by analyzing with Google shortest path.
• This feature also starts quick calls with emergency contacts that are been mentioned by users and the nearest police station, but this can be deactivated with a quick popup screen call screen with a timer to reduce wrong calls if the path is identified by the user.
